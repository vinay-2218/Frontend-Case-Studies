About Indian Premier League (IPL)

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India. It was started in the year 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI).


IPL combines cricket with entertainment and features top players from India and around the world. The tournament is usually held every year between March and May.


IPL is one of the most popular and valuable cricket leagues globally and has changed the way cricket is played and watched.


The league introduced city-based franchises, player auctions, and night matches, making cricket more exciting for fans.
